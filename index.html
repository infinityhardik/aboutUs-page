What is MDF and how is it different from Plywood?

MDF (Medium Density Fibreboard) is an engineered wood product made from wood fibres bonded with resin under high pressure and heat. Unlike plywood, MDF has a smooth, uniform surface with no grain or knots, making it ideal for painting, routing, and precision furniture work. It is available in four grades — from lowest to highest: Interior Grade (standard dry-area use), Exterior Grade (outdoor & semi-outdoor), Super HD+WR / HDHMR (high moisture resistant for kitchens & bathrooms), and TurBo+ (highest density, best moisture resistance, top performance). At Mahadev Traders, we stock Motherwood MDF in all four grades for wholesale supply across Gujarat.

What is HDHMR and Super HD+WR MDF?

HDHMR stands for High Density High Moisture Resistant board — a premium category of MDF engineered for superior density and moisture resistance. Motherwood's Super HD+WR (Super High Density + Water Resistant) is an HDHMR-grade board available in thicknesses of 3, 5.5, 7.5, 11, 16.75, 18, and 25 mm (8×4 ft). It is ideal for kitchen cabinets, bathroom furniture, and high-humidity interior applications across Rajkot, Saurashtra, and Gujarat.

What is TurBo+ Grade MDF and what is Boilo?

TurBo+ is Motherwood's highest density MDF — the top tier in the MDF quality hierarchy, offering the best moisture resistance and product performance of all MDF grades. It is available in 3, 5.5, 8, 12, and 18 mm thicknesses. Boilo is a boiling-water resistance test standard — a board that passes the Boilo test can withstand boiling water immersion. TurBo+ meets this standard, making it suitable for demanding applications including high-humidity environments. The correct MDF grade hierarchy from lowest to highest is: Interior Grade → Exterior Grade → Super HD+WR (HDHMR) → TurBo+ (BEST). All grades are available for wholesale from Mahadev Traders in Rajkot.

What is the difference between BWP and BWR Plywood?

BWP (Boiling Water Proof) is superior to BWR (Boiling Water Resistant). BWP plywood can withstand prolonged exposure to boiling water and is recommended for kitchens and bathrooms, while BWR is suitable for areas with mild humidity. At Mahadev Traders, we provide certified BWP plywood for maximum durability.
