Poplar is lighter and less dense than pine or hardwood, making it suitable for standard applications but not heavy-load furniture. For applications with significant weight or stress, pine wood block board is recommended. Poplar is ideal for cost-conscious projects with normal usage.
With normal indoor use and proper care, poplar block board furniture can last 5-10 years. For temporary or rental applications, it provides excellent value. For longer-term durability, pine wood or hardwood options are better.
Standard poplar block board has basic moisture resistance (MR grade) suitable for normal indoor use. For kitchens or bathrooms with high moisture, waterproof (BWP) block board is recommended, though we also have budget-friendly waterproof options.
