‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Block board is made of rectangular wooden blocks sandwiched between veneer layers. It's stronger and more stable than plywood for large unsupported areas. Block board is ideal for furniture and cabinets, while plywood is better for structural applications.
19mm is lighter and more economical, suitable for shelving and lighter applications. 25mm is heavier and stronger, better for large cabinets and heavy-load furniture. Choose based on span length and load requirements.
We offer poplar (budget-friendly), pine wood (good strength), and hardwood (premium) cores. Poplar is suitable for standard furniture, pine for better strength, and hardwood for high-end applications. Choose based on application and budget.
Standard block board can be used in kitchens with proper protection. For high-moisture areas like bathrooms, we recommend waterproof (BWP grade) block board which is specifically treated for moisture resistance.
Standard sizes are available from ready stock with immediate delivery. For bulk orders, we typically arrange delivery within 5-7 days. For custom specifications, contact our team with your requirements and timeline.
