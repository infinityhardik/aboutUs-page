Liner laminates are thin decorative sheets used for interior surfaces of cabinets and furniture. They create a clean, finished look inside wardrobes, improve light reflection, protect internal surfaces, and add a professional touch. Perfect for visible interiors when doors are open.
White and off-white liners create a sense of spaciousness, reflect light to brighten interiors, and provide a clean, professional appearance. They make cabinets look larger and more organized. Ideal for modern interior design preferences.
